How It Works

Multi-Layer filtering

Our nodes use Path.net and have 14tbit/s of volumetric capacity to filter attacks, bypass floods are then filtered by our L4 XDP mitigation. Clean traffic then goes through our reverse-proxy for intensive checks and to drop L7 floods. This approach has allowed us to mitigate attack records, including 850GBPS of volumetric TCP and 800,000 bots/s.
